Hamburger

This classic homemade hamburger recipe delivers juicy, flavorful burgers that will be the star of any barbecue or family meal. Made with fresh ground beef, seasoned to perfection with a blend of simple spices, and topped with your favorite toppings, this burger is perfect for any occasion. Whether you prefer it loaded with lettuce, tomato, cheese, and onions or customized to your taste, this recipe offers versatility and deliciousness.

The patty is made with high-quality beef, ensuring a tender and juicy bite every time. Grill it to your desired level of doneness and serve it on a soft, toasted bun. Add condiments like ketchup, mustard, or mayo to elevate the flavors, and pair with a side of crispy fries or a fresh salad for a complete meal. Perfect for summer cookouts or a quick weeknight dinner, this hamburger recipe will quickly become a family favorite!

Ingredients list

Ingredients

  • 500g ground beef (80% lean, 20% fat)
  • 1 small onion (finely chopped)
  • 1 egg (optional, for binding)
  • 2 tablespoons breadcrumbs (optional, for binding)
  • 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • Salt and pepper (to taste)
  • 4 hamburger buns (toasted)
  • 4 slices of cheese (optional, for cheeseburgers)
Toppings (Optional):

  • Lettuce leaves
  • Tomato slices
  • Red onion rings
  • Pickles
  • Ketchup
  • Mustard
  • Mayonnaise

Side Suggestions:

  • French fries or sweet potato fries
  • Coleslaw or a fresh salad
