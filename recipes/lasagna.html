Lasagna

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made with layers of pasta sheets, rich meat sauce (often Bolognese), creamy béchamel sauce, and melted cheese. This recipe balances savory flavors, creating a comforting meal that's perfect for a family dinner or gathering.

Ingredients list

Ingredients **For the Meat Sauce:** - 500g ground beef (or a mix of beef and pork) - 1 onion, finely chopped - 2 cloves garlic, minced - 800g canned crushed tomatoes - 2 tablespoons tomato paste - 1 carrot, grated or finely diced - 1 celery stalk, finely diced - 1 teaspoon sugar - 1 teaspoon dried oregano - 1 teaspoon dried basil - Salt and pepper, to taste - Olive oil for cooking - 125ml red wine (optional) - 200ml beef broth or water **For the Béchamel Sauce:** - 50g butter - 50g flour - 500ml milk - 1 pinch of nutmeg - Salt and pepper, to taste **For Assembly:** - 12-15 lasagna sheets (precooked or oven-ready) - 300g mozzarella cheese, shredded - 100g Parmesan cheese, grated - Fresh basil (optional, for garnish)

Cooking Instructions

1. **Make the Meat Sauce:** - Heat 2 tablespoons of olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. - Add the chopped onion, carrot, and celery, sauté until softened (about 5 minutes). - Add the garlic and cook for another minute. - Increase the heat and add the ground beef, cooking until browned and fully cooked (break it up as it cooks). - Once browned, stir in the tomato paste, crushed tomatoes, beef broth (or water), and red wine (if using). - Add oregano, basil, sugar, salt, and pepper to taste. - Reduce heat to low and simmer uncovered for 30-40 minutes, stirring occasionally. The sauce should thicken. 2. **Prepare the Béchamel Sauce:** - In a medium saucepan, melt the butter over medium heat. - Add the flour and stir constantly for about 1-2 minutes to form a roux. - Gradually whisk in the milk, making sure there are no lumps. - Continue cooking, stirring often, until the sauce thickens (about 5-7 minutes). - Season with salt, pepper, and a pinch of nutmeg. - Set aside. 3. **Assemble the Lasagna:** - Preheat your oven to 180°C (350°F). - Spread a thin layer of the meat sauce on the bottom of a large baking dish. - Place a layer of lasagna sheets over the sauce. - Spread another layer of meat sauce over the sheets, followed by a layer of béchamel sauce. - Sprinkle some shredded mozzarella cheese on top. - Repeat the layering process (sheets, meat sauce, béchamel, mozzarella) until you've used up the ingredients. The final layer should be a generous topping of béchamel sauce, mozzarella, and grated Parmesan. 4. **Bake:** - Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil (lightly oiled so it doesn’t stick to the cheese). - Bake for 25 minutes covered, then remove the foil and bake for another 20-25 minutes until the top is golden brown and bubbly. 5. **Rest and Serve:** - Remove the lasagna from the oven and let it rest for 10-15 minutes before slicing. - Garnish with fresh basil leaves if desired.

Enjoy!
