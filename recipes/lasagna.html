Lasagna

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made with layers of pasta sheets, rich meat sauce (often Bolognese), creamy béchamel sauce, and melted cheese. This recipe balances savory flavors, creating a comforting meal that's perfect for a family dinner or gathering.

Ingredients list

Ingredients For the Meat Sauce:

  • 500g ground beef (or a mix of beef and pork) -
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 800g canned crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 carrot, grated or finely diced
  • 1 celery stalk, finely diced
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Olive oil for cooking
  • 125ml red wine (optional)
  • 200ml beef broth or water

For the Béchamel Sauce:

  • 50g butter
  • 50g flour
  • 500ml milk
  • 1 pinch of nutmeg
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

For Assembly:

  • 12-15 lasagna sheets (precooked or oven-ready)
  • 300g mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • 100g Parmesan cheese, grated
  • Fresh basil (optional, for garnish)

Cooking Instructions

**Make the Meat Sauce:**

  1. Heat 2 tablespoons of olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
  2. Add the chopped onion, carrot, and celery, sauté until softened (about 5 minutes).
  3. Add the garlic and cook for another minute.
  4. Increase the heat and add the ground beef, cooking until browned and fully cooked (break it up as it cooks).
  5. Once browned, stir in the tomato paste, crushed tomatoes, beef broth (or water), and red wine (if using).
  6. Add oregano, basil, sugar, salt, and pepper to taste.
  7. Reduce heat to low and simmer uncovered for 30-40 minutes, stirring occasionally. The sauce should thicken.

**Prepare the Béchamel Sauce:**

  1. In a medium saucepan, melt the butter over medium heat.
  2. Add the flour and stir constantly for about 1-2 minutes to form a roux.
  3. Gradually whisk in the milk, making sure there are no lumps.
  4. Continue cooking, stirring often, until the sauce thickens (about 5-7 minutes).
  5. Season with salt, pepper, and a pinch of nutmeg.
  6. Set aside.

**Assemble the Lasagna:**

  1. Preheat your oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Spread a thin layer of the meat sauce on the bottom of a large baking dish.
  3. Place a layer of lasagna sheets over the sauce.
  4. Spread another layer of meat sauce over the sheets, followed by a layer of béchamel sauce.
  5. Sprinkle some shredded mozzarella cheese on top.
  6. Repeat the layering process (sheets, meat sauce, béchamel, mozzarella) until you've used up the ingredients.
  7. The final layer should be a generous topping of béchamel sauce, mozzarella, and grated Parmesan.

**Bake:**

  1. Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil (lightly oiled so it doesn’t stick to the cheese).
  2. Bake for 25 minutes covered, then remove the foil and bake for another 20-25 minutes until the top is golden brown and bubbly.

**Rest and Serve:**

  1. Remove the lasagna from the oven and let it rest for 10-15 minutes before slicing.
  2. Garnish with fresh basil leaves if desired.

Enjoy!
